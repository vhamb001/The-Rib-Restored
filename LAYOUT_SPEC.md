# Layout Specification

## Global Layout

Use a bright white/ivory background with royal blue and gold accents.

### Header

Desktop:
- Left: Logo
- Center/right: Navigation
- Far right: gold “Let’s Connect” button

Header background:
- White or warm white
- Sticky optional
- Thin gold or light gray bottom border

### Logo

The logo should appear as:
THE
RIB
RESTORED

Use gold text.

A short handwritten-style tagline can sit beside it:
“Restored to Purpose. Called to Impact.”

## Home Page Layout

### Hero Section

Two-column layout.

Left:
- Welcome to
- The Rib Restored
- Restored in script gold
- By Shalaymah
- short intro
- buttons

Right:
- bright author-style image
- royal blue and gold paint accent on the far right
- optional quote card:
  “For such a time as this, I write.”

Hero should feel light, spacious, and royal.

### Quick Explore Section

5 cards in a horizontal row:
- Books
- Writings
- Projects
- About Me
- Contact

Each card:
- icon
- heading
- short sentence

Use white cards with royal blue icons and gold hover.

### Newsletter Strip

Royal blue background with gold script heading:
“Stay connected. Be the first to know.”

Fields:
- Name
- Email
- Subscribe button

### Featured Books

4-column grid on desktop, 2-column tablet, 1-column mobile.

Each book card:
- cover image
- title
- short description
- button

### Latest Writings

List layout:
- thumbnail image
- title
- excerpt
- date
- read more link

### Projects Preview

Card layout with icons:
- The Rib Restored
- Beit Yahweh HaGo’el
- Listen Selah
- Future Projects

### Contact Preview

Optional on homepage or only on contact page.

## Mobile Layout

- Header becomes hamburger menu
- Hero stacks vertically
- Explore cards become 2-column or single column
- Book cards stack
- Footer stacks vertically

## Buttons

Primary button:
- royal blue background
- white text
- gold hover or gold border

Secondary button:
- white background
- royal blue border
- royal blue text

Gold button:
- gold background
- white or dark text
- subtle shadow
