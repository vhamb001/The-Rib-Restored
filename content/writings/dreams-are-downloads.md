Many dismiss dreams as mere echoes of the day’s anxieties or random firings of the mind. But for the prophetic dreamer, the night is a canvas for divine communication. Dreams are not just visions; they are downloads of kingdom understanding.

When we sleep, our flesh rests, but our spirit remains awake. It is in this state that instructions, warnings, and revelations are often deposited into our hearts. The key is not merely to dream, but to remember and interpret. Keep your scroll close by. Write down what you see. The imagery of a watchtower, an unrolling scroll, or a starry sky—none of these are accidents.

A single dream, properly understood, can alter the course of a life, provide strategy for a season, or birth a new level of restoration. Pay attention to the night. It is speaking.
